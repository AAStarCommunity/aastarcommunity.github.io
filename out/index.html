What is AAStar?


AAStar^ is a subgroup of Plancker^ community. We launched AAStar after a conversation with Vitalik at Zuzalu, Montenegro in 2022.
We will commit to the Ethereum for long term contribution and construction. We will focus on Account Abstraction in Ethereum Roadmap.
Mission: Empower Community with Ethereum AA.
Vision: A better digital future for humanity with Simple Account by AAStarÔºÅ
Value: A Gift for Human from AAStar Hackers.

What are we building?


  • SuperPaymaster: A simple gas payment framework utilizing ERC4337 and EIP7702.

  • AirAccount: An easy interface for your Ethereum account life cycle.

  • Comet ENS: A meaningful name for your addresses in Ethereum.

    • Our Features

    Credential Binding

    Bind any credential: Web2 accounts, EOAs, Email, SMS.

    Secure Account Generation

    TEE-based encrypted account generation and management on ARM.

    Double Verification

    Fingerprint and TEE-based secondary verification with enhanced AI security checks.

    Full Account Lifecycle Management

    AA-based management: social recovery, will execution, daily limits, etc.

    Gasless & Seamless Transactions

    Intuitive Web2-like experiences (payments, tips, likes, NFTs) powered by AA.

    True Decentralization

    Decentralized accounts with fingerprint + TEE + secure computation validators for user data sovereignty.

    Ultra Security

    Layered security solutions tailored for different user needs.

    dApps Portal

    Curated list of dApps supporting EIP1271 and experimental EIP7702 applications.
